Quick Answer

Is My Winter Car out?

Yes. My Winter Car is available and playable now as an Early Access game.

When did it come out?

The Steam release date is December 29, 2025. That is the Early Access release, not a final 1.0 date.

What was the last update?

The latest official Steam news item tracked for this page is UPDATE v.260415-01, dated April 15, 2026.

Release date vs full release

For My Winter Car, most players asking "when is My Winter Car coming out" mean the playable Steam launch. That already happened. A separate final 1.0/full-release date has not been treated as the same thing on this page.

Current Status

My Winter Car is playable, but it is still an Early Access game. That means the foundation is available now, while content, polish, fixes, balance, and quality-of-life changes can continue through updates.

  • Playable now: yes, through Steam Early Access.
  • Final version: not the focus of the current public status.
  • Updates: active update history exists after launch.
  • Best next step for new players: start with the Beginner's Guide after checking the status here.

Release Timeline

May 29, 2020

My Winter Car was publicly announced during the My Summer Car era.

November 28, 2025

The Steam store page became available before launch, giving players a central place to wishlist and follow the game.

December 29, 2025

My Winter Car released on Steam Early Access. This is the main release date players should use when asking when the game came out.

January-April 2026

Post-launch updates continued with fixes, feature additions, and gameplay adjustments. Full version-by-version details belong in the Patch Notes archive.

Last Update Snapshot

The latest official Steam news item tracked for this page is UPDATE v.260415-01 from April 15, 2026. It includes fixes and additions such as tire wear data fixes, packaging job fixes, lottery functionality, motor oils, cash deposit at ATM, bus driving improvements, and an ice scraper.

FAQ

When did My Winter Car come out?

My Winter Car came out on Steam Early Access on December 29, 2025.

Is My Winter Car fully released?

No. It is playable now, but the public status is Early Access. Treat mechanics, balance, and features as subject to change.

When is My Winter Car coming out on full release?

This page does not list a final 1.0 date because the confirmed public date players can act on is the Steam Early Access release date: December 29, 2025.

What is the latest update?

The latest official Steam news item tracked here is UPDATE v.260415-01, dated April 15, 2026. For the complete update history, read Patch Notes.

Should I buy now or wait?

Buy now if you want the Early Access version and are comfortable with bugs and changing mechanics. Wait if you only want a more finished version with less surprise from future changes.
