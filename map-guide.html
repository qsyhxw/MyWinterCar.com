In-Game Physical Map

Where to find it

The physical map is at the parents' house, above the telephone table in the main room. It is the most immersive way to check the whole region without relying on a modern dashboard GPS.

How to read it

  • Red thick lines: paved highway routes.
  • Thin dark lines: smaller dirt roads.
  • Purple buildings: commercial or public buildings.
  • Black buildings: houses and residential stops.
  • Brown or marshy areas: avoid with normal vehicles.

Best habit

Call job or sale phone numbers before driving when possible. Some objectives add map markers, which makes long-distance navigation much easier.

Route Planning

Apartment to PSK

Use this as your basic supply loop. Buy food, fuel, and check job board activity before longer drives.

PSK to Fleetari

Plan around shop hours and cash. If Fleetari keeps the vehicle, have a ride, loaner, or walking plan ready.

Town to Parents' House

Use the safer road route while learning; switch to lake shortcuts after you understand braking distance and traction.

Lake Shortcut

Best when avoiding highway cameras or saving time, but the surface rewards gentle steering and early braking.

FAQ

Is this a real interactive map?

Yes. The markers, filters, and detail panel are interactive. The layout is schematic because an exact official coordinate map is not available for safe embedding.

Why is the layout schematic?

The goal is fast navigation, not a coordinate-perfect scan. Use it to learn route relationships, then confirm exact turns with the physical in-game map.

Does My Winter Car have GPS?

No modern dashboard GPS should be expected. Use the in-game physical map, visual landmarks, and phone-generated markers.

What should I learn first?

Memorize apartment, PSK, parents' house, Fleetari, Pub Nappo, and the lake shortcut. Those locations cover most early needs.
