üí° Key Information

My Winter Car features 4 distinct income sources: one main factory job offering steady weekly paychecks (2910 MK/week), and three freelance positions with flexible schedules. Your choice determines not just income, but your entire gameplay rhythm. This guide provides verified phone numbers, application procedures, and income optimization strategies based on v.260102 mechanics.

üè≠ Factory Job - Complete Application Guide

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Warning

If you get fired, you CANNOT reapply. Missing 2-3 work days or repeatedly slacking (clocking in but not working) will permanently lock you out. You'll also lose unemployment benefits for 60-90 days. Always clock in AND complete work.

üìç Location & Application Process

  • Find the Factory: Drive to the large red building (Futufon/Teffelgren Oy) on the highway southeast of town, near Loppe exit. Look for red industrial building with company signage.
  • Park & Prep: Park in the lot and immediately plug your vehicle into the block heater outlet. Batteries die fast in winter temperatures.
  • Enter & Find Reception: Walk through main door, turn LEFT. Speak with receptionist (not Suski). She'll direct you to the boss's office.
  • Interview Process: Press K to progress dialogue. Boss will take phone calls, walk around factory, ask questions. DO NOT hang up or leave him. Spam K when needed.
  • Sign Contract: After the tour, boss presents contract. Sign it, shake his hand. You start the following Monday at 07:00.

üìÖ Work Requirements

Standard Hours: Monday-Friday, 07:00-17:00 (8-hour minimum)
Sliding Hours: Arrive at 08:17? Leave at 16:17. The clock adjusts.
Overtime: Work beyond 8 hours accumulates. 8 overtime hours = 1 day off
Mandatory Meeting: Every Tuesday in presentation room (next to break room)
Punch Clock: MUST clock in/out or you don't get paid
Supervision: Boss monitors efficiency. Clocking in without working = grounds for termination

üéØ Work Tasks

  1. Grab pile of flat cardboard boxes from workstation
  2. Fold one box at a time (pieces snap together like car parts)
  3. Insert plastic tray + charging cable + manual into box
  4. Place completed box on delivery pallet
  5. Repeat until shift ends (or until pallet is full)

üíµ Salary Progression

Period Weekly Pay Notes
Week 1-2 (Trial) 2470 MK Probationary period - reduced pay
Week 3+ 2910 MK Full pay after successful trial
Week 5+ 3710 MK Maximum salary (continues working)

Payment auto-deposits into bank account. Taxes automatically deducted (counts as official income for traffic violations).

üöï Taxi Job - Complete Driver's Manual

‚ö†Ô∏è One-Time Meeting Warning

If you call and don't show up at PSK at 12:00, or don't enter the passenger seat, the taxi driver disappears permanently. You cannot get the job again. After calling 08-712112, you MUST meet him the next weekday at exactly 12:00 (noon) at PSK parking lot.

üîì Unlocking the Taxi Job

  • Prerequisites: Buy the Corris Rivett from Reijo (call 08-609553, pay 500 MK). Drive or transport Rivett to your parents' house. Job flyer appears on PSK notice board only after Rivett is at parents' house.
  • Find the Flyer: Go to PSK gas station restaurant area, check notice board on wall near entrance. Note phone number: 08-712112
  • Make the Call: Return to your apartment, use phone to dial 08-712112. WAIT until he finishes speaking and hangs up first. Do not hang up early or job won't activate.
  • The Meeting: Go to PSK parking lot the next WEEKDAY at exactly 12:00. Taxi driver will be waiting in Machtwagen. Enter the PASSENGER seat.
  • Training Session: Listen to entire briefing. He explains taximeter, modes, procedures. This is your only training. After dialogue ends, you get the taxi.

üöó The Machtwagen (Taxi Vehicle)

Real-World Basis: Mercedes-Benz W124 200D (pre-facelift 1985-89)
Engine: Diesel (accepts diesel or fuel oil - but fuel oil = police ticket risk)
Transmission: 4-speed manual, RWD
Features: ABS, digital thermometer, car phone, taximeter, receipt printer
Mileage: 470,000 km when you get it (still in "great shape" per taxi man)
Fuel Tank: 70 liters, cap on right rear side
Radio: VLPANE aftermarket CD player
Handling: Similar to Ferndale - high torque, RWD, spins easily on dirt roads

‚úÖ Pro Tip: Handling Improvement

Place sandbags (found at parents' house) in the trunk to improve traction and reduce spin-outs on icy roads. Makes winter driving significantly safer.

üîÑ Typical Taxi Call Workflow

  1. Wait for Call: Set taximeter to Minus mode, red light ON. Phone will ring with customer request.
  2. Accept Call: Listen to ENTIRE dialogue. Customer gives pickup address. If you interrupt, customer may not spawn.
  3. Prepare Meter: Turn red button OFF, set knob to Mode 1 (day) or Mode 2 (night/weekend).
  4. Pick Up Customer: Drive to address (check sun visor map). Customer enters vehicle with luggage.
  5. Drive to Destination: Customer tells you where to go. Fare increases automatically while driving.
  6. Arrive & Stop Meter: Turn red button back ON (pauses fare).
  7. Process Payment: Press button to print receipt. Transfer displayed amount via payment terminal. Amount MUST match meter exactly or you lose money from personal account.
  8. Complete Transaction: Hand receipt to customer if requested. Unload luggage. Ready for next call.

üí∞ Taxi Income Breakdown

Your Cut: 40% of displayed fare + kilometer allowance
Weekend Bonus: Mode 2 rates (32 MK base) give better earnings
Maximum Calls: 8 customers per day
8 Unique Locations: Different pickup/dropoff points (check sun visor map)
Payment: Immediate per ride, no weekly paycheck system

‚ùå Common Mistakes That Cost Money

  • Using wrong mode (Mode 1 at night or Mode 2 during day) = undercharging customer
  • Forgetting to turn red button OFF when picking up passenger = no fare accrual
  • Receipt amount doesn't match meter = you pay the difference from personal funds
  • Not listening to full customer dialogue = customer doesn't spawn, wasted trip
  • Driving too aggressively = car flips/slides easily when entering (RWD + ABS = tricky)

üó∫Ô∏è Finding the Taxi Map

Once you receive the taxi, enter driver's seat and flip down the sun visor. A regional map is stored there showing all 8 customer pickup/dropoff locations with address markers. This map also displays a reminder chart for which fare modes to use during different times.

ü™µ Firewood Delivery - Best Income/Hour Job

üîì Activation Requirements

  • Buy the Rivett: Call Reijo at 08-609553 (number also on PSK notice board). Listen to entire call, wait for dart icon on map. Drive to marked location (same spot as firewood buyer in My Summer Car).
  • Purchase Price: 500 MK for bare Rivett frame with 10-12 random parts. You need this purchase to activate firewood job.
  • Automatic Activation: After buying Rivett, Reijo will call YOU when he or residents need firewood. You can also call him proactively.

üöú Equipment Setup - Kekmet 502 & Wood Cutter

Location: Large red woodshed at parents' house (Kesselinper√§ peninsula). Kekmet tractor + hydraulic wood splitter + trailer are stored here.

  1. Start the Kekmet: Enter tractor cab. Pull hand throttle lever. Turn ignition key to start diesel engine.
  2. Position Tractor: Reverse Kekmet toward wood cutter so rear of tractor faces cutter attachment point.
  3. Connect Hitch: Pull rear hitch lever (right side of driver's seat) to hook up the cutter.
  4. Attach PTO Shaft: Exit tractor. Connect drive shaft under tractor rear to cutter input. This powers the hydraulic splitter.
  5. Engage PTO: Return to cab. Activate PTO using lever left of steering wheel.
  6. Maximize RPM: Pull hand throttle to maximum position. Higher RPM = faster log splitting, less time in cold.

ü™ì Wood Processing & Loading

  • Find Logs: Barn storage near woodshed has log pile. Grab logs with F key.
  • Feed Cutter: Push logs into hydraulic splitter input. Machine splits and drops firewood into trailer automatically.
  • Fill Trailer: Continue until trailer is full (160 pieces for complete load, earns maximum payment).
  • Efficiency: With high RPM, entire process takes 30-45 minutes including loading.

üöö Delivery Process

  1. Hitch Trailer: Ensure trailer is connected to Kekmet rear hitch.
  2. Drive to Customer: Reijo's house OR resident who called (check map for dart icon). Customers only outside 08:00-22:00.
  3. Position for Unload: Reverse trailer near delivery area. Look for green tarpaulin on ground.
  4. Raise Trailer Bed: Use rear hydraulics lever to tilt trailer upward.
  5. Open Rear Hatch: Release trailer gate to dump firewood.
  6. Critical: Dump on Green Tarp! Firewood MUST land on tarpaulin or "slightly beside it". Dumping on regular ground = no payment, customer won't accept.
  7. Receive Payment: Customer immediately gives 2000-3200 MK cash depending on load size.

üìç Known Delivery Locations

  • Reijo's House (Firewood guy / Rivett seller)
  • Strawberry Farm
  • House next to Per√§j√§rvi bus stop
  • House between hayball farm and Ventti house driveway
  • Additional residents will call when they need deliveries

üí∞ Income Analysis

Payment Range: 2000-3200 MK per load (typically 3200 MK for full 160-piece load)
Time Investment: 30-60 minutes total (processing + delivery)
Effective Hourly Rate: ~3200-6400 MK/hour (best in game!)
Frequency: On-demand, residents call every few days
Stacking Potential: Process multiple loads, store in trailer, deliver when called

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes

  • Delivering to wrong house or when no resident is outside = wasted load, no payment
  • Dumping firewood off tarpaulin = customer rejects, no payment
  • Forgetting to engage PTO = cutter won't work, wasted time
  • Low RPM operation = extremely slow splitting, freezing hazard

üéØ Pro Strategy

This is widely considered the BEST early-game job. After buying Rivett (500 MK initial investment), you can earn 3200 MK in under an hour. Many players skip factory job entirely, combining Firewood + Advert Delivery + occasional Taxi runs for maximum freedom and income. Process firewood in batches during good weather, deliver when calls come in.

üì¨ Advert Delivery - Beginner-Friendly Income

üîì Activation (Simplest Job)

  • Find Number: Go to PSK gas station restaurant, check notice board for yellow poster. Phone number: 08-231206
  • Make the Call: Use any phone (apartment, parents' house, PSK), dial 08-231206.
  • Wait for Completion: Listen to ENTIRE call. Do NOT hang up early or job won't activate. Man will hang up on you.
  • Confirmation: If call successful, advert pile appears at apartment doorstep next Monday morning.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Bug & Fix

Problem: Adverts don't appear at doorstep after calling.
Cause: Call ended prematurely or game didn't register activation.
Solution: Save game, exit completely, restart. Try calling again and wait for full dialogue.

üì¶ Collection & Delivery

  1. Collect Pile: Stack of 30 advert sheets appears at apartment door every Monday. Take 27 for delivery (3 spares in case you lose any).
  2. Pick Up Individual Sheet: Look at pile, press F to take ONE sheet at a time. You carry one per trip to mailbox.
  3. Find Mailboxes: 27 total mailboxes scattered along main roads throughout Alivieska. Same locations as My Summer Car (except player's own mailbox is excluded).
  4. Delivery Method: Drive to mailbox, hold advert sheet, hover cursor over mailbox until hand icon appears. Click LMB to insert.
  5. Route Planning: Follow main road through town, hit each house. Mailboxes are visible at front of properties.

üíµ Payment System

Payment Amount: 459 MK for all 27 mailboxes (maximum)
Partial Delivery: Payment reduces for each missed mailbox
Payment Day: Every Friday (in-game)
Payment Method: Auto-deposit to bank account, listed as "Jakopalkkio"
How to Check: Go to PSK ATM, check balance/transactions. Look for Jakopalkkio entry.

üó∫Ô∏è Mailbox Locations (14 Areas, 27 Mailboxes)

Starting clockwise from player's apartment, mailboxes are located at:

  • Houses along main road from Per√§j√§rvi toward town
  • Residential properties near PSK gas station
  • Houses past Fleetari's repair shop
  • Properties toward Kesselinper√§ peninsula
  • Scattered rural homes between major locations
  • Note: Some locations have multiple mailboxes (2-3 per property)

üéØ Strategy & Tips

‚úÖ Optimization Guide

  • Timing: Deliver anytime during the week, no rush. Adverts appear Monday, payment Friday.
  • Vehicle Choice: Use Sorbet (your starting car) - no special vehicle needed. Consumes minimal fuel.
  • Weather: Deliver during clear weather for safety. Job isn't time-sensitive.
  • Route: Follow main roads, don't take shortcuts. Mailboxes are roadside, easy to spot.
  • Combining Tasks: Perfect to stack with other errands (parts shopping, visiting Fleetari, etc.)
  • Weekly Routine: Set up Monday routine: collect adverts, deliver throughout week, check ATM Friday.

üéÆ Why This Job Matters

While 459 MK/week isn't huge, this job is critical for beginners:

  • Zero Prerequisites: No vehicle purchase, no equipment, no interview
  • Map Learning: Forces you to explore Alivieska, learn layout, find key locations
  • Guaranteed Income: Unlike taxi (variable) or firewood (on-demand), this is predictable
  • Stack with Everything: Doesn't conflict with factory hours or other jobs
  • First 500 MK Goal: Two weeks of adverts = enough to buy Rivett and unlock firewood job

‚ö†Ô∏è Cost Awareness

Hidden Expenses That Eat Income

  • Fuel: 100-300 MK/week depending on vehicle and job (diesel cheaper than gasoline)
  • Food: 50-100 MK/week (groceries from PSK or Pub Nappo)
  • Beer: 50-150 MK/week (mandatory for Problem meter management, character is alcoholic)
  • Rent: Auto-deducted from bank every other week (amount varies)
  • Car Repairs: Variable (battery 400 MK, tires 300 MK each, engine parts 1000-5000 MK)
  • Fines: Speeding tickets 500-1500 MK, fuel tax violation 1000 MK
  • Heating: Electricity for block heater usage (minimal but adds up)

Survival Buffer: Always keep 1000-1500 MK liquid cash for emergencies (battery dies, car breakdown, unexpected fines).

üèÜ Advanced Money-Making Tips

  • Bottle Hunting: Return bottles to PSK (2 MK each). Dozens scattered around map = easy 50-100 MK.
  • Flea Market Flipping: Buy cheap used parts Saturday mornings, install/test, resell at profit.
  • Ventti Card Game: Play at pub (gambling) - risky but can win big. Some players report 10000+ MK gains.
  • Scrap Metal: Items around parents' property can be sold. Check sheds, barns for sellable junk.
  • Unemployment Benefits: If avoiding factory job, you receive housing allowance + benefits (~300 MK covers rent).
  • Factory Overtime: Work 10+ hours/day early in week, accumulate 8+ overtime hours = free day off, still get paid.
  • Taxi Weekend Focus: Mode 2 rates (32 MK base) on Saturday-Sunday = higher fares, max 8 calls/day = 16 calls weekend.
  • Batch Firewood Processing: Spend one full day processing 3-4 trailer loads, deliver throughout week as calls come.
