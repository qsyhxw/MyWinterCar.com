üí° Pro Tip: Auto-Clutch

If manual transmission is too hard, enable auto-clutch in settings (F1). This removes the need to use X when shifting gears.

‚ö†Ô∏è NUMPAD Required for Phone

The number row (1234567890) does NOT work for phone calls. You MUST use the NUMPAD on the right side of your keyboard. If you don't have a numpad, you may need to remap controls.

üöó Starting the Sorbett - Complete Tutorial

The Sorbett 1600 LTD (based on Talbot 1510) is your daily driver. It's parked outside your apartment, already plugged into a block heater. Here's how to start it properly:

CRITICAL: Block Heater Management

üî¥ ALWAYS Unplug Before Driving!

If you drive with the block heater cable attached, you will rip it out and damage your car. Always unplug at the FRONT of the car before getting in.

When parked at home: ALWAYS plug it back in! If left unplugged overnight, the oil freezes and the car won't start.

Cold Start Procedure

  1. Approach the Sorbett
    Go to the front of the car, find the block heater cable
  2. Unplug block heater
    Right-click the cable, drop it on ground
  3. Get inside
    Press C to crouch, click driver door. Windows will be frozen - this is normal.
  4. Enter driving mode
    Press Enter. You'll see "N" (neutral) in bottom-right corner.
  5. Insert key
    Hover over ignition, click ONCE with LMB. Dashboard lights turn on.
  6. Pull choke
    Look to LEFT of steering wheel, find small knob. Click with LMB to pull it OUT fully.
  7. Start engine
    HOLD LMB on ignition while giving a little gas (W). Engine will sputter and struggle.
  8. Let it idle
    Once running, let engine idle for 1-2 minutes. After driving a bit, slowly push choke back IN with RMB.

Defrosting Windows

Your windshield will be covered in ice. Two methods:

Method 1: Heater (Slow but thorough)

  • Window Heater button: Square button on right dashboard. Press until yellow light turns on.
  • Blower: Click button TWICE for maximum power (you'll hear it get louder)
  • Temperature: Mouse scroll on temperature dial ‚Üí Set to HOT (all the way left/red)
  • Direction: Adjust toggle so arrows point toward windshield
  • Wait: Idle 3-5 minutes. Ice will slowly melt.

Method 2: Manual Scraping (Fast but incomplete)

  • Exit car, approach windshield from outside
  • Hover over frozen glass until you see "Scrape Window" prompt
  • Hold LMB and move mouse UP and DOWN repeatedly
  • Clear driver's window and left side of windshield (minimum to see)
üí° Recommended: Combine Both Methods

Start heater first, then manually scrape while it warms up. Gives you 50% visibility in 1-2 minutes instead of waiting 5 minutes.

Driving Basics

  1. Release handbrake: Look at handbrake lever, click or press Space
  2. First gear: Press G to shift into 1st
  3. Accelerate: Press W (yes, even in reverse you use W!)
  4. Steer: A and D
  5. Shift up: As RPMs increase, press G for 2nd, 3rd, etc.
  6. Brake: S to slow down
  7. Shift down: B when slowing or need more power
‚ö†Ô∏è Ice Roads Are Extremely Slippery

Drive slowly, especially on turns. Brake BEFORE corners, not during. Use engine braking (downshift) instead of brakes when possible.

üìû Buying the Corris Rivett - Step-by-Step

The Corris Rivett (based on Ford Taunus TC) is your main project car. It costs 500mk and requires precise steps to purchase.

Where to Find the Ad

Drive to PSK gas station. Inside, look for the job board on the right wall (past the automatic sliding doors). You'll see an advertisement with a phone number.

üí° Phone Number

The Corris Rivett seller's number is: 08609553

You can skip the PSK visit and call directly from home if you already know the number.

Making the Call (CRITICAL STEPS)

  1. Go to apartment phone
    Phone is in the hallway. Pick up receiver (LMB).
  2. Dial using NUMPAD
    Type: 08609553
    MUST use NUMPAD! Number row doesn't work. Press Enter after typing.
  3. Wait for seller to talk
    He'll ramble about the car. DO NOT hang up!
  4. Wait for HIM to hang up
    You'll hear the line go dead / beeping tone. If you hang up early, the quest won't trigger!
  5. Check your map
    Open map (in apartment). You should see a new "Car for Sale" pin at location 9.
üî¥ Common Failure: Hanging Up Early

This is the #1 mistake. If you hang up before the seller finishes talking, the map marker WON'T appear. You'll have to call again. Be patient!

Visiting the Firewood Guy

  • Location: Map marker shows "Car for Sale" (position 9 on map)
  • When to visit: DAYTIME ONLY - He won't appear at night
  • What to bring: 500mk cash (from bank account works too)
  • How to get there: Drive Sorbett or use Kekmet tractor

When you arrive:

  1. Approach the seller (Reijo Livaloinen, aka "Firewood Guy")
  2. Wait for dialogue to complete
  3. When prompted, pay 500mk
  4. Car is yours!

What You Get

The Corris Rivett comes as:

  • Empty shell - Just the body/frame
  • 10-12 random parts - Lying on ground nearby
  • NO ENGINE - You must build everything
üí° Moving the Rivett

The Rivett cannot be driven (no engine!). You need to TOW it:

  • Kekmet tractor - Safest, found at woodshed by parents' house
  • Gifu truck - Faster but harder to control

Many players tow it to parents' house instead of apartment because of the motor hoist in garage.

‚ùÑÔ∏è Survival Basics - Don't Freeze to Death

My Winter Car is set in January 1999 Finland. Temperature is around -24¬∞C. Cold kills.

Critical Stat: Body Temperature

üî¥ Hypothermia Warning

Without heat sources, hypothermia can kill you quickly. Never venture outdoors without the winter jacket!

Staying Warm:

  • Winter jacket: Equip immediately (floor near apartment door, press F)
  • Apartment radiators: Turn on in every room, set to 20-30¬∞C
  • Car heater: Use when driving (see Sorbett section above)
  • Limit outdoor time: Get in/out of car quickly

Sleeping & Saving

‚ö†Ô∏è Game ONLY Saves When You Sleep

There is NO autosave. The ONLY way to save is:

  • Sleep in your bed (apartment or parents' house)
  • Apartment must be warm (30¬∞C radiator minimum) or sleep won't work

Save frequently! Death = lose all progress since last sleep.

üí° Hidden Quick Save

Pressing P (urinate) also triggers a quick save. Use this when you're far from home and want to save progress.

Money Management

  • Bank account: You start with one. Use ATM at PSK to check balance.
  • PIN code: First PIN you enter is PERMANENT. Remember it!
  • Rent: 525mk/week automatically deducted from bank account
  • Cannot deposit cash: In EA version, no way to put physical cash into bank
  • Jobs pay into bank: Office job, delivery jobs deposit directly

‚ö†Ô∏è Top 10 Beginner Mistakes - Avoid These!

‚ùå Mistake #1: Forgetting to Unplug Block Heater

Result: Ripped cord, damaged car
Prevention: ALWAYS unplug at front of car BEFORE driving. Make it a habit!

‚ùå Mistake #2: Hanging Up Phone Early

Result: Corris Rivett quest doesn't trigger, map marker won't appear
Prevention: Wait until you hear beeping tone / line goes dead. Be patient!

‚ùå Mistake #3: Using Number Row Instead of NUMPAD

Result: Phone calls don't work, orders fail
Prevention: ONLY use NUMPAD for all phone dialing. Number row (top of keyboard) doesn't work.

‚ùå Mistake #4: Apartment Too Cold to Sleep

Result: Can't save game, lose progress
Prevention: Set bedroom radiator to 30¬∞C. Leave on at least half the day.

‚ùå Mistake #5: Driving Without Defrosting

Result: Crash, death, loss of progress
Prevention: ALWAYS scrape or heat windshield before driving. Zero visibility = guaranteed crash.

‚ùå Mistake #6: No Headlights at Night

Result: Crash or police fine
Prevention: Press L to turn on headlights after sunset. Check dashboard indicator.

‚ùå Mistake #7: Visiting Firewood Guy at Night

Result: Wasted trip, fuel, time
Prevention: Only visit during daytime. He won't spawn at night.

‚ùå Mistake #8: Not Plugging In Block Heater Overnight

Result: Oil freezes, car won't start in morning
Prevention: ALWAYS plug in block heater when parking at home overnight.

‚ùå Mistake #9: Forgetting to Save

Result: Hours of progress lost on death
Prevention: Sleep in bed regularly. Game ONLY saves when you sleep. Use P for emergency quick save.

‚ùå Mistake #10: Wrong First PIN Code

Result: Locked out of bank card
Prevention: First PIN you enter is PERMANENT. Choose something you'll remember. No way to reset in-game!

üÜò Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I play with a controller?

A: Controller support exists but is limited in EA. Many actions (like scraping windows) are easier with mouse. Keyboard is strongly recommended.

Q: What if I don't have a NUMPAD?

A: You'll need to remap phone controls or use an on-screen keyboard. NUMPAD is required for phone calls in default settings.

Q: How long does it take to build the Rivett?

A: With 100+ parts needed, expect 20-30 hours of real-time work for a first build. Experienced players can do it in 10-15 hours.

Q: Should I enable permadeath?

A: NO for first playthrough! Permadeath deletes your save on death. Learn the game with normal mode first.

Q: Why can't I sleep in my bed?

A: Apartment is too cold. Turn on bedroom radiator to 30¬∞C and wait for room to warm up.

Q: The Sorbett won't start. Help!

A: Did you plug in the block heater overnight? If car was left unplugged, oil froze. See Troubleshooting Guide.
