5. Driving Tips

Master the Sorbett with these helpful tips:

üßä Cold Weather Starts

Use the block heater before cold starts. Plug it in when parked near an outlet to ensure easy starting in extreme cold.

üå°Ô∏è Cabin Heat

Turn on the interior heater while driving to maintain body temperature. The heater works better when the engine is preheated.

The 58L tank is generous, but keep an eye on fuel levels. Don't get stranded in the Finnish wilderness!

üöó FWD Handling
