‚úÖ Content Quality Policy

Every guide on mywintercar.com follows a mandatory quality assurance process to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Our Fact-Checking Workflow

Before any guide is published, we complete a comprehensive fact-check report that includes:

üìã Fact-Check Requirements
  • Multi-Source Verification: Every claim must be verified by at least 2 independent sources
  • Source Hierarchy: We prioritize official wiki ‚Üí developer statements ‚Üí community consensus
  • Confidence Scoring: Each piece of information receives a confidence rating (0-1.0 scale)
  • Timestamp Requirements: All evidence must include dates/version numbers for time-sensitive data
  • EA Uncertainty Warnings: Information with confidence < 0.7 receives explicit warning boxes

What We DON'T Publish

  • Single-source claims without verification
  • Speculation or theories presented as facts
  • Information from sources older than 12 months (unless re-verified)
  • Game data that cannot be independently confirmed
‚ö†Ô∏è Early Access Disclaimer

My Winter Car is in Early Access. Game mechanics can change with any patch. When you see yellow warning boxes in our guides, it means that specific information has lower confidence or may be subject to change. We update our guides as the game evolves.

Handling Conflicting Information

When sources disagree, we document both perspectives and label them clearly. We never make arbitrary decisions about which source is "correct" without testing or community consensus.

Design Philosophy

Our dark winter theme with ice-blue accents and warm warning tones reflects the harsh Finnish environment of the game. The color palette was carefully chosen to:

  • Reduce eye strain during extended reading sessions
  • Create clear visual hierarchy with color-coded information boxes
  • Maintain brand consistency across all 8+ guides
  • Support both desktop and mobile reading experiences
üéØ Performance First

Every page is a self-contained HTML file with embedded CSS. No external stylesheets, no JavaScript frameworks, no database queries. This approach ensures:

  • Sub-second page load times
  • Works offline after first visit
  • Compatible with low-bandwidth connections
  • Easy to archive and maintain long-term
