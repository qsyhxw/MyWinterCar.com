💡 What is the Bachglotz?

If you're asking "what is the bachglotz mwc players keep talking about?", you've come to the right place. The Bachglotz is a classic American land yacht based on the 1965 Oldsmobile 98 Holiday Coupe. Within our complete vehicles guide, it stands out as the primary loaner vehicle. Players can borrow it from Fleetari's repair shop when their main project car (the Corris Rivett) is left in for tuning, inspection, or repairs. As one of the most prominent vehicles in the mwc bachglotz lineup, driving this heavyweight beast on icy roads is both a luxury and a massive challenge.

🔑 How to Borrow the Bachglotz

The Loaner Mechanics

The Bachglotz isn't your property. It belongs to Fleetari, the local mechanic. To drive it legally (without upsetting Fleetari), you must follow these steps:

  1. Drive your Corris Rivett (or towed project car) to Fleetari's repair shop in Loppe.
  2. Order a service from his catalog (e.g., body repair, engine tuning, wheel alignment).
  3. Wait for Fleetari to take your keys (you will be left stranded).
  4. The keys to the Bachglotz will normally be available on his counter or the car will be unlocked outside as a temporary loaner.

⚠️ Fleetari's Patience

Do not steal the Bachglotz! If you take the vehicle without leaving your car for a legitimate service order, or if you fail to return it after Fleetari calls you to pick up your finished Rivett, the mechanic will seek revenge. Historically, Fleetari might dump your prized project car into a swamp or strip parts if you cross him.

❄️ Winter Handling & Black Ice

Driving the bachglotz on the National Route 70 during the Finnish winter is terrifying. The sheer mass of the vehicle means that once it begins to slide on white or black ice, recovering is nearly impossible. You cannot use clutch-kick techniques to save yourself because it is an automatic. Treat the brake pedal like glass.

⛽ Fuel Consumption

Being an American V8 from the 1960s, the Bachglotz is incredibly thirsty. If you borrow it for an extended period to do adverts or taxi runs, you will spend a massive portion of your profits simply refuelling at the PSK Gas Station. Always check the fuel gauge before driving off.

💡 Community Tips & Quirks

  • The Floating Boat: Due to extremely soft American suspension, the Bachglotz leans heavily in corners. Do not attempt high-speed cornering on dirt roads, or it will roll over into the ditch.
  • Starting the Engine: Like other cars in the winter simulation, you may need to pump the gas to prime the carburetor before turning the key. If it's left outside Fleetari's shop in -20°C overnight, it will struggle to crank.
  • Aesthetic Appeal: The interior offers a wide bench seat. While you cannot modify the Bachglotz like you can with the Rivett, it provides a very immersive "cruiser" experience with the game's radio on.
