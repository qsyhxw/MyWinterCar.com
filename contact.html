Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact the developer directly?
Developer Johannes Rojola (Toplessgun) primarily monitors the Steam Community Forums. For official support, use the Steam platform. He occasionally participates in Discord discussions but Steam is the primary channel.
Is this wiki official?
No, mywintercar.com is a community-created resource independent of the game developers. We follow strict fact-checking protocols and cross-reference information with official sources. For official information, always check the Steam Store page and developer announcements.
Where can I find the latest game updates?
Check the Steam Community Hub for official patch notes. The developer posts major updates in the Steam News section and discusses upcoming features in the forums.
How can I contribute to this wiki?
We welcome contributions! Join our Discord community to discuss potential contributions. All submissions must include sources and follow our fact-checking standards outlined in the About page.
Can I use wiki content in my videos/streams?
Yes! Our guides are free to reference in videos, streams, and other content. We appreciate attribution but it's not required. Just don't claim you wrote the guides yourself. See our About page for more details.
Why isn't Reddit listed as a community platform?
As of January 2026, there is no established official or large-scale My Winter Car subreddit. The primary community hubs are Discord and Steam Forums. If a dedicated subreddit emerges, we'll add it here.
