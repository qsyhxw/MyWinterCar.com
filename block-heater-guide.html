1. What is a Block Heater?

A block heater (also known as an engine preheater) is an essential device for cold weather driving in Finland. It keeps your engine warm when parked, making cold starts much easier and protecting your engine from extreme cold damage.

üîå

Engine Block Heater

Warms the engine block and coolant using external electrical power. Essential for reliable cold starts in Finnish winters!

Feature Details
Power Source External electrical outlet
What It Heats Engine block and coolant
Engine State Works with engine OFF
Cable Location Front of the car
üîã How It Works

The block heater is powered by external electricity, not your car battery. This means you can leave it plugged in overnight without draining your battery. The heating element warms the engine block and coolant, keeping everything at an optimal temperature for starting.

2. How to Use the Block Heater

Using the block heater is straightforward, but following the correct steps is crucial:

1

Park Near an Electrical Outlet

Drive your car close enough to an electrical outlet so the block heater cable can reach. Make sure there's enough space to access both the cable and the outlet.

2

Locate the Block Heater Cable

Find the block heater cable at the front of your car. It should have a plug that's accessible from the engine bay area or grille.

3

Connect the Cable to the Outlet

Grab the cable plug and connect it to the electrical socket. The heater will start working immediately.

4

Turn Off the Engine

The block heater works while the engine is OFF. You can leave it plugged in while you sleep or do other activities in the game.

5

Unplug BEFORE Driving!

CRITICAL: Always disconnect the cable before trying to start or drive your car. Forgetting this step will cause problems!

6

Start Your Car

With the cable disconnected, turn the ignition. The preheated engine should start almost immediately, even in extreme cold!

üå°Ô∏è Cold Start Problems Without Preheating

In extreme cold, your engine oil becomes thick and sluggish. Without preheating, your car may:

  • Struggle to start or fail to start entirely
  • Take a long time to warm up
  • Have weak cabin heating initially
  • Experience increased engine wear

4. Important Warnings

üö® NEVER Forget to Unplug!

You MUST disconnect the block heater cable before attempting to drive. If you forget:

  • Your car will not start or move correctly
  • You may damage the cable or outlet
  • The cable could get tangled or ripped off
