‚ö†Ô∏è Don't Wait for Multiplayer

If you're considering buying My Winter Car specifically because you want to play multiplayer with friends, do not buy the game.

There is no official multiplayer, no confirmed plans for multiplayer, and the developer has explicitly stated it won't be added. The game is designed as a singleplayer experience.

üîç Why Is There No Multiplayer?

Technical Reasons

üíª Engine & Code Complexity
  • Unity 5 Engine: MWC uses Unity 5, an older engine not optimized for networking
  • Code Architecture: Game was built from the ground up as singleplayer ‚Äî adding multiplayer would require rewriting all core systems
  • Synchronization Challenges: Syncing 200+ car parts, physics simulation, and AI across multiple clients is extremely complex
  • Solo Developer: Toplessgun develops alone (with wife Kaarina on support) ‚Äî multiplayer would require significant team expansion

Design Philosophy Reasons

üé® Intentional Singleplayer Vision
  • Personal Journey: The game is about your struggle to survive Finnish winter and build a car
  • Loneliness Theme: Developer quote: "MWC is going to be great, but it's going to be lonely as hell" ‚Äî this isolation is intentional
  • Progression Balance: Game systems are balanced for solo play ‚Äî multiplayer would break resource/money/job mechanics
  • Permadeath Mode: Hardcore difficulty designed for single-player commitment
üìä Community Feedback

From players who tried MSC multiplayer mods: "There is mod in my summer car for coop, it works but it's like 5/10, quite a meh."

Reality: Even unofficial mods struggle to make multiplayer work smoothly due to game complexity. Multiple players reported mods being "broken," with sync issues, physics glitches, and crashes.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will multiplayer be added in a future update?
A: No. The developer has explicitly stated multiplayer will never be added to the game. This is not a "maybe later" situation ‚Äî it's a firm "no."
Q: Can I play with friends using Steam Remote Play?
A: Technically yes, but it's not true multiplayer ‚Äî one person controls while others watch. This is a Steam feature, not game-specific, and provides a poor experience for simulation games.
Q: Is there local split-screen co-op?
A: No. There is no split-screen or local co-op mode.
Q: Did My Summer Car have multiplayer?
A: No. MSC was also singleplayer only. Community mods exist but are described as low-quality ("5/10") with frequent bugs and sync issues.
Q: Are multiplayer mods safe to use?
A: Use at your own risk. Mods can corrupt saves, break with updates, and are not supported by the developer. Always backup your save files before installing ANY mod.
Q: Why does the game not have online leaderboards?
A: This is unconfirmed. Some community members speculate online leaderboards for race times might be added, but nothing has been officially announced.
Q: Can I convince the developer to add multiplayer?
A: No. Toplessgun has been asked this question for years and his answer remains the same. The game is intentionally designed as a singleplayer experience. Please respect the developer's vision.
Q: If I buy the game hoping for multiplayer later, will I be disappointed?
A: Yes, absolutely. Do not buy this game expecting multiplayer to be added. It won't be. Buy it because you want a singleplayer Finnish winter survival simulator.
