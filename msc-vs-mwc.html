‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL: Rivett Has No Rev Limiter!

Major Change from Satsuma: The Corris Rivett has NO electronic rev limiter. If you over-rev the engine (especially during "money shifts" - accidental downshifts at high RPM), you will cause catastrophic engine damage.

This is particularly dangerous with tuned engines. MSC veterans used to bouncing off the rev limiter must break this habit immediately.

Car Building System Changes

üîß Rewritten Build System
IMPROVED

NEW in MWC: Can buy multiple copies of the same part. This enables spare parts, easier replacement, and provides ~20% better FPS during assembly.

In MSC, you could only have one of each part type at a time.

üí• Increased Breakable Parts
HARDER

50-60% of parts can break in MWC vs lower percentage in MSC. Parts show visual wear states (scuff marks) and make sounds indicating when replacement is needed.

Suspension parts break easily on heavy hits if in bad condition.

üîç VIN System
NEW SYSTEM

Every Rivett has unique VIN determining factory spec: build location, trim level, engine, transmission, axle ratio, paint color, interior, optional equipment.

Check VIN plate before ordering parts to match your car's specification!

‚ùÑÔ∏è Survival Mechanics: From Forgiving to Relentless

üö® Biggest Change: Survival is Now THE GAME

MSC: Survival was a background mechanic. Hunger and thirst were easily managed. Mistakes hurt but rarely ended runs.

MWC: Survival is relentless and unforgiving. Temperature, alcohol dependency, and time management are constant threats. You can freeze to death in 10-15 minutes without proper clothing.

NEW: Temperature & Cold System

ü•∂ Body Temperature System
NEW IN MWC
  • Death Timer: 10-15 minutes outdoors without proper gear
  • Clothing Layers: Indoor vs outdoor gear required
  • Building Heat: Houses/apartment need heating
  • Sleep Restrictions: Can't sleep if room too cold
  • Vehicle Heaters: Only work when engine coolant warm + controls set correctly
üç∫ Problem Bar (Alcohol Dependency)
REPLACES THIRST
  • NOT thirst: Represents alcoholism/addiction
  • Water doesn't help: Only alcohol reduces it effectively
  • Controversial mechanic: Creates dependency loop
  • Starting state: Character begins as alcoholic (empty bottles in apartment)
  • Management: Kilju brewing or buying alcohol
üî• Heating & Energy Management
NEW SYSTEM
  • Rent & Utility Bills: Must pay for apartment heating
  • Sauna Heating: Takes much longer due to freezing temps
  • Car Block Heaters: Must plug in overnight or engine oil freezes
  • Indoor/Outdoor: Can't use some furniture (sofa) outside due to cold
‚ö†Ô∏è Problem Bar Controversy

Most confusing mechanic: The Problem Bar has confused thousands of players who think it's thirst. It's NOT. Water barely helps. This is intentional ‚Äî your character is an alcoholic.

This design choice is controversial but deliberate. Developer Toplessgun stated this mechanic explores addiction themes in Finnish rural culture.

üèÜ Final Verdict: Which One Should You Play?

‚òÄÔ∏è Play My Summer Car If You Want:

  • Freedom & Exploration: No time pressure, play at your own pace
  • Easier Entry Point: Forgiving survival, gentler difficulty curve
  • Complete Experience: Fully released game with all features
  • First-Time Finnish Simulator: Learn mechanics without harsh punishment
  • Casual Building: Car parts mostly provided, less grinding

‚ùÑÔ∏è Play My Winter Car If You Want:

  • Hardcore Challenge: Relentless survival, punishing difficulty
  • Structured Gameplay: Time management, job schedules, adult responsibilities
  • New Survival Systems: Temperature, clothing, alcohol dependency
  • MSC Mastery Test: Explicitly designed for MSC veterans
  • Winter Atmosphere: Dark, depressing, beautiful Finnish winter

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warnings:

  • MWC is NOT for beginners. Developer explicitly warns: "for players who already master My Summer Car"
  • MWC is in Early Access. Still has bugs, missing features (ice race rewards, historical inspection accuracy, etc.)
  • Temperature system can feel unfair. Death in 10-15 minutes requires constant vigilance
  • Problem Bar mechanic is controversial. Alcohol dependency loop is intentional but divisive
  • Getting fired is permanent. Unlike MSC's side jobs, you can lose your main income source

üéØ Our Recommendation:

Start with My Summer Car. Master the core mechanics (car building, survival, controls) in a forgiving environment first.

Then move to My Winter Car once you:

  • Can build Satsuma from scratch without guides
  • Understand all survival mechanics
  • Are comfortable with MSC's jank and difficulty
  • Want a fresh, harder challenge in the same universe
